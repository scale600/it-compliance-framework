# References & Standards

> **Purpose**: Comprehensive list of regulatory texts, standards, frameworks, and tools referenced throughout this compliance framework.

---

## Laws & Regulations

| Reference | Full Title | Jurisdiction | Relevance |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| **GDPR** | General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679) | EU / EEA | Core privacy regulation; lawful bases, Data Subject rights, processor obligations, breach notification |
| **CCPA/CPRA** | California Consumer Privacy Act / California Privacy Rights Act (as amended by CPRA) | California, USA | PI categories, consumer rights, Service Provider obligations, Sensitive PI |
| **UK GDPR** | UK General Data Protection Regulation | United Kingdom | Post-Brexit equivalent of EU GDPR; requires UK-specific SCC Addendum for transfers |
| **ePrivacy Directive** | Directive 2002/58/EC (Cookie Law) | EU | Cookie consent requirements; complements GDPR |
| **HIPAA** | Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act | USA | Not directly applicable to manufacturing unless handling employee health data; reference for health data protection |

## International Data Transfers

| Mechanism | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC)** | EU 2021/914 — four modules for Controller-Controller, Controller-Processor, Processor-Processor, Processor-Controller transfers |
| **EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF)** | Successor to Privacy Shield; requires self-certification by US companies |
| **UK International Data Transfer Addendum** | UK-specific addendum to EU SCCs effective March 2022 |
| **Schrems II Ruling** | CJEU C-311/18 — invalidated Privacy Shield; requires Transfer Impact Assessment for SCC-based transfers |
| **Binding Corporate Rules (BCR)** | Intra-group transfer mechanism for multinationals (requires DPA approval) |
